3. Static

  1. Using the WinTherm-Thermal Analytics Software, imput desired temperature into “set temp” box and press enter.
  2. Submerge apparatus into water bath for 200 minutes.

4. Hardening

  1. Using the WinTherm-Thermal Analytics Software, input first desired temperature into “set temp” box and press enter.
  2. Submerge apparatus into water bath for 60 minutes.
  3. Remove the apparatus for 5 minutes
  4. Input second desired temperature into “set temp” box and press enter.
  5. Re-insert the apparatus into the water bath for 200min.
